Hi! I'm Aneetej. I'm currently an undergraduate student at The Ohio State University, where I recently successfully defended my Honors Undergraduate Thesis in AI interpretability. I will graduate in May 2026 Summa cum laude.

My primary interests are in machine learning foundations, AI interpretability, AI safety, and the governance of AI. I care deeply about the long-term impacts of AI and how we can steer it towards positive outcomes for humanity.

I previously worked at the Johns Hopkins Applied Physics Lab as a machine learning intern. Before this, I was a student researcher at the Machine Intellgience and Data Science Laboratory (MIDS) at The Ohio State University. I have also been a student researcher at the Harvard ALARM laboratory. Outside of work I enjoy playing squash, hiking, listening to music, and weightlifting.
